our menu popular menu

Nous Nous

This is a signature Moroccan coffee, which translates to “half-half.” It’s made with equal parts of espresso and steamed milk, served in a small glass. It’s stronger than typical Moroccan coffees and is a must-try for coffee enthusiasts visiting Morocco

$2.99

Café Noir / Black Coffee

A small cup of espresso, this is a straightforward and strong coffee option, often preferred by locals. It’s perfect if you enjoy a milder coffee flavor.

$2.99

Qahwa Ma’atra

A spiced coffee that includes a blend of spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, and nutmeg, ground together with the coffee beans for a warm and aromatic flavor.

$3.99

Espresso

The concentrated essence of coffee, extracted by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans. It’s the foundation for many other coffee drinks.

$3.99

Macchiato

An espresso with a dash of milk or foam. It’s bold and intense, perfect for those who appreciate the coffee’s true character.

$4.99

Latte

A comforting concoction of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam. Customize it with flavored syrups for a delightful twist.
